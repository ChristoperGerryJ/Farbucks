About Us

  Created by William Thaddeus and Christoper Gerry Johanes on 2 June 2022 at their own house. The beginning of the formation of FArbucks Coffee was due to the gap between price, quality, and coffee maker which caused anxiety for both of us to enjoy coffee. For us, coffee is not just ground bean mashed with water, it is an art to create a taste that goes into every customer.

  Farbuck was created in 2 sides, namely the coffee side and the market side. Coffee brewed at FArbucks is not just adding water to coffee grounds, but our inovation is making coffee with art that adapts to the taste buds of the customers so that the taste of coffee at Starbucks is not constant for one perspective only. The market we saw was also very large because coffee has become part of the basic needs of many people, therefore among all coffee shops, we try to provide something different for customers.
